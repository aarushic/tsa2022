Use Your Power

Legislative action is very important in the fight against climate change, so you can write to or call your representative to urge them to take action. Here are some pieces of legislation currently in Congress that you can write to your representatives about. Note: This section only provides information for US residents. But, if you live somewhere else, feel free to contact your own local and national representatives.

H.R.2310 - Climate Change Education Act

This bill, if passed, would require the administrator of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) to establish a Climate Change Education Program. It was introduced in the House on 4/01/2021.

Bill Text: https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/2310/text

8H.R.2826 - To establish a Global Climate Change Resilience Strategy, to authorize the admission of climate-displaced persons, and for other purposes.

This bill, if passed, would require the administrator of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) to establish a Climate Change Education Program. It was introduced in the House on 4/01/2021.

Bill Text: https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/2826/text

Feeding the World

Giving back to the world we live in is so important because it has given so much to us. So, we partnered up with one of your favorite restaurants, Garden Dome, to raise money for charities that give back to nature.

One way to support the fight against deforestation and all the negative consequences that come from it is to fund the organizations actively fighting against it. What better way to do this than by partnering with an environmentally-friendly organization?

So go to your nearby Garden Dome and mention our event and all your expenses will be donated to the charity of your choice! And as a bonus, If you spend over $50, you win a free handmade bracelet!

This is only active during this 4-day event, so don't miss the opportunity to help us reach our overall goal of $500,000 in donations!
